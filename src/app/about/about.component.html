Pegasus Consulting was incorporated in 2015 to bring a new dimension into the education arena, with the combined experience of the founders in the education, industry and technology spheres. Based in Bangalore, these individuals have over 100 man years of combined experience, gained both in India and internationally.

​

The company has set its sights on becoming an international player of repute in providing custom business solutions using IT to deliver a rich end-user experience in the education domain.

​

The company has built a solid ecosystem of technology and education partners and intends to create a multi-channel go-to-market strategy to deliver quality educational content to not just the urban populace but also provide the same quality of content to the upcoming and socially challenged segments of society.

​

The promoters have an innate belief in the philosophy of equality of education and intend to deliver the content free of cost to those who are in true need. Giving back to society and accepting social responsibility for sharing knowledge remains a firm tenet of the organization.
